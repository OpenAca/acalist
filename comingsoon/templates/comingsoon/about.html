About the Acalist

The Acalist is an effort by Craig Martek to organize the online world of a cappella. There are three primary goals of the Acalist:

  • Make it easy to locate the a cappella groups in a given area.
  • Give greater visibility to acappella events.
  • Help those looking to join a group find either auditioning groups or other local people that might want to start a new group.

Would you like to help out? Feel free to get in touch!

The Acalist is graciously supported by the fine folks at The Vocal Company.
